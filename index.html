About Me

Hello! My name is Tyson Lackey. I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon and have lived here most of my life. There were a couple of years spent at Oregon State University in Corvallis, but other that, Metro Portland has always been home. Lacrosse was a huge passion of mine growing up and I played through middle school and high school. I decided to stop playing competitively after graduating so I could focus on the full college experience instead of being consumed by sports 24/7. I was pursuing a business degree from Oregon State and Portland State depending (bouncing between the two from 2008-2011) before deciding that a 4-year university wasn't for me. After leaving school and a few years of working at a netflix call center, a career in Business Intelligence focused in the Logistics field kind of fell into my lap. Working as a junior analyst taught me most of my background in SQL, Power Query, and Power BI. A few years into that field, I realized that I wanted to cement a better foundation in my development skills and possibly pursue a new career in coding. If nothing else, learning code would supplement a career in BI better than any on the job tricks I would pick up along the way. Fate seemed to notice my shift in interests and my job was outsourced in August of 2019 creating a perfect opportunity to dive head first into this new field and skillset. If I'm not working, I'm almost always playing video games. While I have a passion for travel and the outdoors, recent events have definitely forced more of my recreation onto the computer or television. Luckily, golf courses have been quick to implement strict social distancing rules and remain open. As the weather continues to improve, I plan on spending as much time as possible on the links trying to shake off almost a year's worth of rust.
